Villagarnival: The 7th southern China regional meeting for iGEMers

Southern China regional meeting is an annual special event held by us SZU-China. We've successfully held the meetings 7 times since 2017.


The southern China regional meeting aims to gather iGEM teams in southern part of China and to bridge a stage for teams to present projects and interact with each other. On this stage we built, teams can draw on the experience of each other and cooperate together to solve problems. With all these moves, teams here with us will manage together to form the atmosphere of discovering the secret of Synthetic Biology.



This time the meeting's topic is "villagarnival", which represents "village-carnival". We entitle the meeting with this name to correspond with the newly-set concept of "villages" this year and to show our initial heart to promise teams to purely enjoy the world of Synthetic Biology. The 7th southern China region meeting for iGEMers focus on being more interactive and interesting to bulid a delighting platform for iGEMers to have brainstorms.

IGBA: The 1st iGEM great bay area Synthetic Biology Industry-Academia research forum


The 1st iGEM great bay area Synthetic Biology Industry-Academia research forum is an industry-academia regional forum jointly hold by the BNZHU-China, UM, HKUST, SZU-China, aims to provide a platform based on face-to-face communication for iGEM teams and related enterprises in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao great bay area. As one of the most densely distributed areas of the iGEM teams and bio-related high-tech industries in the world, we four universities in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao great bay area have joined together to open up a new way of iGEM meetup, and take the industry-academia linkage as the core of the forum, combining the industries and academies in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao great bay area. Innovation poles and growth poles are gathered together to deeply strengthen regional bonds and friendly cooperations.

In this forum, we gain the heat point in the field of Synthetic Biology and learn the core techs of many newly-found biotech companies. Also, we improve our project design by comunicating with teams in Hong Kong and Macau.

10th CCIC: 10th conference of China iGEMer community


Conference of China iGEMer community is the national meeting launched by the iGEM teams in China aimimg to provide a platform for resource sharing for teams and Chinese young synthetic biology enthusiasts to promote mutual learning and communication. Since in 2014 it was held for the first time, the CCiC Conference is focusing to maintain the sustainable development of the community and Synthetic Biology in China. The platform plays an important role in all walks of life in industry, university and research, and has received a good response. At present, the CCIC Conference has become one of the largest academic conferences for college students in China.

In the conference, we communicate on many modules such as hardware and modeling with many teams. Also, we attend workshops to grasp the potentials of Synthetic Biology.

BNUZH-China

With BNUZH-China, we focus on how to perform a better series of HP activities. We mention about how we do campus activities and learn from BNUZH-China about how to arrange better education and design related materials.

ZJU-China

Together with ZJU-China, we focus on the discussion of how to organically combine two types of plant immunity PTI and ETI to better design our project to realize a more practical product.



As an important power in southern part of China, we SZU-China promise to keep promoting teams' connections and flourishing the development of Synthetic Biology. We hold the faith that with efforts of many more teams, Synthetic Biology will glimpse its new era!
