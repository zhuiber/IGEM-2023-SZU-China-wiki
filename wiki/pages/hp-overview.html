Laws Analysis and Standard proposal for PolycoBead


During the course of the project, we have observed a misalignment between new technologies and existing laws.In order to bridge the gap between new products, existing laws, and the current market, we have conducted a legal analysis and proposed industry standards.

"Legal Analysis - Investigation of PolycoBead Application under Existing Laws"
We have extensively studied legal literature related to products and technologies, examining the applicability of existing laws to emerging products. Simultaneously, we have organized the acquired knowledge into a comprehensive reference, wrote the "Legal Analysis - Investigation of PolycoBead Application under Existing Laws". This work will serve as a robust legal guide for other research teams and companies.

"The Proposal of Chinese RNA Pesticide Industry Standard "
After discovering the mismatch between new products and existing laws, we proposed industry standards relying on our previous experience and social observations during our human practice work, aiming at bridge the gap between the new product and existing market. Additionally, we sought to prepare in advance for the influx of emerging products and foster a market order that is standardized and orderly. This proposal has received positive recognition from the Shenzhen municipal government.




From Unit to Union – T.O.M.A.T.O Alliance


By conducting comprehensive investigations into all every unit involved in pesticide usage, including famers (pesticides users), traditional pesticide productors, biotech companies who provides technical support and governments, we have proposed the concept----From Unit TO Union. Starting from relevant units, we aim to connect each group and form a unified union----T.O.M.A.T.O Alliance, which includes TOMATO-Technical Alliance(Technology-Oriented Modernization of Agriculture-Targeted Organization) and TOMATO-Thought Alliance(Thought-Oriented Modernization of Agriculture-Targeted Organization). Through the collaboration of various units in the market and society, the TOMATO Alliance aims to pave the way for market adoption and societal awareness of RNAi biopesticides with powers of the whole union.
