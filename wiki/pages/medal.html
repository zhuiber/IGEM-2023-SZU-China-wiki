Project Attributions On the attributions page, we have acknowledged the roles and tasks fulfilled by each team member in the lab and for the wiki. Further, we appreciated all those who supported and assisted us with our project. Find our Attributions page.
Project Description Our project description page provides a comprehensive outline of the project and inspiration. We have also provided visuals to explain our project. Visit our Description page.
Project Contribution Our team designed and characterized multiple new parts, including RNAi and plant immunity. In order to reduce the production cost of shRNA, we have designed a large-scale in vitro transcription device for shRNA as our hardware. We also have developed the China Industrial Standard for RNAi Pesticide and established the Enterprise Alliance T.O.M.A.T.O. to make breakthrough in iHP part in the village of Agriculture and promote the development of industry. With above contributions, we hope to provide ideas for the iGEM teams in the village of Agriculture. Read more on the Contribution page.

Awards

We are incredibly honored and grateful to have been a part of this year's IGEM competition. As a team of 13 dedicated individuals, we poured our hearts and souls into this project for an entire year. The journey was challenging, but the experience was truly invaluable.

Standing on the stage in Paris and being recognized as a top 10 team, along with receiving the Best Agriculture Project award, was a moment we will never forget. It validated all the hard work, late nights, and endless dedication we put into our project.

This experience has enriched each of us in ways that extend far beyond the competition itself. We've learned the true meaning of teamwork, perseverance, and innovation. It has not only broadened our scientific knowledge but also shaped us as individuals.

We would like to express our deepest gratitude to the IGEM organization, our PI & Instructors, advisors, and supporters who believed in us every step of the way.
